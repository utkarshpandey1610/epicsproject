Services

Bath & Dry

Our Bath & Dry service includes a thorough wash with high-quality pet shampoo, a conditioning treatment to keep your pet's coat shiny and healthy, and a gentle blow-dry to ensure that your pet stays warm and comfortable.

Styling

Our styling services include haircuts, trims, and styling for all types of pets, including dogs, cats, and more. We use only the best quality products, and our experienced stylists take the time to ensure that your pet is comfortable and relaxed throughout the entire styling process.

Full Grooming

Our full grooming service includes a bath with high-quality pet shampoo, a conditioning treatment to keep your pet's coat healthy and shiny, a thorough brushing to remove any tangles or mats, and a gentle blow-dry to ensure that your pet stays warm and comfortable.

Medical Bath

Our medical bath service uses specialized shampoo and conditioning products that are specifically formulated for pets with sensitive skin or medical conditions, such as allergies, dermatitis, or fungal infections.

Fun Activities

fun activities include daily walks and outdoor adventures that allow pets to explore new sights and smells and interactive games and puzzles that help to stimulate your pet's mind and keep them mentally sharp.

Veterinary

At Welcoming Paws, we understand that your pet's health is of the utmost importance. That's why we have a close relationship with local veterinarians and can provide referrals to trusted veterinary clinics in the area.

Vaccines

Our vaccine services are tailored to your pet's individual needs and are administered in a safe and comfortable environment

Training

At Welcoming Paws, we believe that every pet has the potential to be a well-behaved and obedient companion. Our training programs are designed to help your pet reach their full potential, and our experienced trainers will work with you every step of the way to ensure that you and your pet achieve your training goals.
